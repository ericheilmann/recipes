Ingredients:
- 1 pound beef
- Cooking oil
- red curry paste
- Thai fish sauce
- 1 can coconut milk
- raw sugar
- basil

Slice beef thinly and brown in pan with cooking oil.

When almost brown, add 1 spoon of red curry paste can continue to brown until complete.

Add fish sauce, then coconut milk and allow temperature to come back to simmer. Add sugar to taste. Clean and add basil leaves shortly before serving.

Serve over steamed rice.
